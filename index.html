About Us

Wudleaf Interior Solutions is a leading interior company based in Malappuram district, Kerala began with furniture manufacturing and gradually upgraded to assist in total interior solutions. With an experience of over 20 years of working on 1000+ projects across Kerala, we assure you a better choice of sustainable furniture with supreme quality, innovative designs, and elegant style. We never compromise in providing the best-customised furniture and customer service that has gained the trust of our clients all over Kerala. The company employs a diverse workforce of skilled workers and carpenters from the production unit, expert designers, and site supervisors who guarantee to bring the best output that exceeds the client's imagination. We are proud to have these highly professional and committed team members to manufacture quality-assured products and implement positive change. The innovative approach utilising the company's modern pieces of equipment to produce super-comfortable furniture enables it to increase efficiency and remain competitive in today's ever-changing trends in the market. Our expertise lies in impeccable craftsmanship to create authentic and classy furniture made with passion in our factory outlet. Being long-lasting and trend-setter collections, you can choose accessories to accommodate the most diverse interiors of the residential and cooperative sectors. Despite an increasingly competitive business environment, we promise to create the best value for the product that delivers true satisfaction to our customers. The customer is the king. Therefore, we aim to give the best choice after planning and modifying it according to the client's suggestions and budget. Engagement with the clients is significant when choosing the suitable material, design, theme, and colour, evaluating the dimensions, and manufacturing the accessories. We help you shape your dream design and assist it in installation into the space within the provided time too.
